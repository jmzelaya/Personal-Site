About Me

Hello! I am Jamilet Zelaya, a recent Ironhack graduate who used to teach 8th grade Science. In 2015 I graduated Magna Cum Laude from FAU with a BA in Psychology. Originally wanting to pursue a career in Social Work to dedicate myself to troubled youth, I worked at a school to gain experience with children. However, after starting as a Substitute, then a Co-Teacher, and finally as a full-time Teacher I discovered my passion for Web Development and decided to pursue it as a career. So I continued to teach throughout the school year and worked towards an opportunity at Ironhack's Web Development bootcamp. After 9 long weeks of learning I never looked back.
